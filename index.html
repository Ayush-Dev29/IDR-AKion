About IDR

Where academia meets the operational edge

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment organisation built for one purpose: producing capable, credible digital risk professionals ready for regulated, high-consequence environments. We exist at the intersection of rigorous academic thinking and the messy, urgent reality of cyber, technology and AI risk in practice.

In partnership with a leading UK university, IDR designs and delivers programmes that are both theoretically grounded and relentlessly practical. Our graduates don't just understand risk frameworks — they know how to apply them under pressure, within complex organisations, and alongside the people who depend on them most.

What We Do

Three pillars, one purpose

Everything IDR does feeds into a single pipeline: training practitioners, developing ideas, and deploying both where they're needed most.

The Academy

Bootcamps, structured programmes and professional development tracks for students and working practitioners. Curriculum spans digital risk fundamentals through to advanced AI governance and technology risk in regulated sectors.
